ODRL Validator Demo

This demo validates ODRL policies, ensuring that they conform to the ODRL Information Model 2.2. The policy is first normalized, then checked against a set of SHACL constraints. If the policy satisfies these structural checks, a final reasoning step looks for conflicts in the policy.

Validation results are grouped into three categories. After validating a policy, you can expand each reported finding to view additional details.

  • Violation: The policy does not conform to the ODRL Information Model.
  • Warning: The policy can still be interpreted, but it relies on constructs that are incomplete or omitted and must be inferred.
  • Conflict: The policy is structurally valid, but contains rules (or other constructs) that contradict one another.
